For Creators: How It Works
No more manually DMing everyone who comments. Here's how to set up your DropReveal profile and let the platform do the work.
Step 1 — Sign Up
Head to www.dropreveal.com and create your creator account. Set up your profile with your name, handle, and a short bio. Your profile URL will look like: dropreveal.com/your-handle
Step 2 — Add Your Reels
For each reel you've posted where you promised a resource, create a card on DropReveal. You'll need:
•	The reel ID or link from Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts
•	A short title so viewers can search for it (e.g., "ChatGPT Prompt for Emails")
•	Optional: a thumbnail or cover image
Step 3 — Attach Your Resources
For each reel card, you can add any combination of the following:
•	AI Prompt — paste your prompt text directly. Viewers click "Reveal Prompt" to see it.
•	Downloadable File — upload your workbook, PDF, checklist, or template. Viewers click to download.
•	Shop / Product Links — add one or more links to products, Gumroad pages, Amazon listings, or your own store.
•	Custom Links — any other URL you want to share with your audience.
Step 4 — Share Your Profile
Once your profile is live, just update your reel captions and bio with your DropReveal link:
"Comment PROMPT or visit dropreveal.com/your-handle to get it instantly!"
That's it. Viewers come to your profile, find the reel, and access the resource themselves.
What Can I Share?
•	AI & ChatGPT prompts
•	Notion templates and Google Sheets
•	PDF guides, eBooks, and workbooks
•	Links to your Etsy, Gumroad, or Shopify store
•	Affiliate product links
•	Course or community links
•	Any URL you want your audience to visit
Tips for Best Results
•	Use clear, searchable titles for your reel cards so viewers can find them quickly.
•	Update your bio link to point to your DropReveal profile.
•	Pin your DropReveal link in your reel comments for extra visibility.
•	Add all your past reels — not just future ones. Viewers often go back to older content.
Need Help?
We're happy to help you get set up. Email us at support@dropreveal.com and we'll walk you through it.


Frequently Asked Questions
For Viewers
Is DropReveal free to use?
Yes. Browsing creator profiles and accessing resources on DropReveal is completely free for viewers.
Do I need an account to access resources?
No. You can browse creator profiles and reveal resources without creating an account.
How do I find the reel I'm looking for?
Visit the creator's DropReveal profile and either scroll through their reels or use the search bar to look up by keyword or reel ID. The reel ID is usually the code at the end of the Instagram or TikTok URL.
The creator said to comment but I found this profile — is this legit?
Yes! Creators share their DropReveal profile as a faster, simpler alternative to the comment-and-DM method. Instead of waiting for a manual DM, you get the resource instantly here.
What if the resource I'm looking for isn't on the creator's profile?
The creator may not have added that reel yet. Try commenting on the original reel and letting the creator know you'd like it added to their DropReveal profile. You can also contact us and we'll flag it.
Can I share the resources I download?
Resources are shared for your personal use. Redistribution or reselling without the creator's permission is not allowed. Check the creator's individual terms if they specify usage rights.

For Creators
Is DropReveal free for creators?
We offer a free tier that lets you add a limited number of reel cards. Paid plans allow unlimited reels and additional features. Check our pricing page for current details.
Which platforms are supported?
You can link reels from Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. We're working on adding more platforms.
How do viewers find my profile?
Share your DropReveal profile link in your bio, reel captions, and comments. Your URL will be dropreveal.com/your-handle.
Can I add resources to old reels I've already posted?
Absolutely. You can add any of your past reels to DropReveal at any time. Viewers who discover your profile will be able to access resources for older content too.
What file types can I upload?
You can upload PDFs, Excel/Google Sheets exports (.xlsx), Word documents (.docx), images (.jpg, .png), and zip files. Maximum file size is 50 MB per upload.
What happens if I delete a reel on Instagram or TikTok?
Your DropReveal card will still exist and resources will still be accessible. The original reel preview may not load, but the content you attached will remain available.
Can I password-protect certain resources?
This is a feature we're actively building. For now, all resources on a public profile are accessible to anyone who visits your page.
I'm not tech-savvy. Is this hard to set up?
Not at all. Most creators have their profile live within 15 minutes. If you get stuck at any point, email us at support@dropreveal.com and we'll walk you through it personally.
